# Installing VidX as a PWA (Progressive Web App)

## Benefits
- **Hides address bar** on both iOS and Android
- **Full-screen immersive experience**
- **Offline support** for cached pages
- **Home screen icon** like a native app

## Installation Instructions

### iOS (Safari)
1. Open Safari and navigate to your VidX site
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
5. Open the app from your home screen - no address bar!

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open Chrome and navigate to your VidX site
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add"**
5. Open the app from your home screen - no address bar!

## What's Included
- ✅ Standalone display mode (no browser UI)
- ✅ Service worker for offline caching
- ✅ App manifest with theme colors
- ✅ Mobile-optimized meta tags
- ✅ Works on both iOS and Android

## Note
For full functionality, you'll need to add app icons to `/images/`:
- `icon-192.png` (192x192px)
- `icon-512.png` (512x512px)

You can generate these at: https://www.pwabuilder.com/imageGenerator
